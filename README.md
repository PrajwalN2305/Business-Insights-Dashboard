# Business-Insights-Dashboard

## Overview

An interactive Business Insights Dashboard developed using Power BI to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and operational efficiency using the Superstore dataset.

The dashboard enables business users to monitor key performance indicators, identify profitable product categories, track customer trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* SQL
* Microsoft Excel
* DAX
* Data Modeling

---

## Dataset

* Superstore Dataset

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

Key business performance metrics including:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin %
* Total Orders
* Average Discount
* Return Rate

Visualizations:

* Monthly Sales Trend
* Year-over-Year Sales Comparison
* Sales by Category
* Revenue Distribution by Region
* Dynamic Year Filter

---

### 2. Product and Category Analysis

Product performance and profitability analysis.

Visualizations:

* Profitability Breakdown by Category and Sub-Category
* Sales Distribution Across Product Sub-Categories
* Profit vs Sales Quadrant Analysis
* Top 10 Revenue-Generating Products

---

### 3. Customers & Operations

Customer and operational performance analysis.

Visualizations:

* Top 10 Customers by Revenue
* Average Delivery Time by Ship Mode
* Year-over-Year Sales Growth Trend
* Sales Distribution Across States

---

## Key Insights

* Total Sales exceeded $2.3M.
* Technology category generated the highest revenue.
* Phones emerged as the top-performing product sub-category.
* Profitability varied significantly across product categories.
* Standard Class shipping showed the highest delivery time.
* Customer revenue concentration identified high-value customers.
